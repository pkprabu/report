3. Step-by-Step Workflow

Step 1: Open & Edit
Open the `.html` file in your browser. Click on any text or number to edit it. Use the control panel at the top to add/remove rows or sections.

Step 2: Select All
Once finished, click anywhere on the page and press Ctrl+A (or Cmd+A on Mac) to select everything.

Step 3: Copy
Press Ctrl+C (or Cmd+C on Mac) to copy the entire selection.

Step 4: Paste into Outlook
Create a new email in Outlook. In the message body, press Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on Mac) to paste. The report will appear perfectly formatted.
